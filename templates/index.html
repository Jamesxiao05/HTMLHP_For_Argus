Dynamic HTML Content Delivery

Serving tailored content to different clients.

Welcome! You've reached the public-facing page of our advanced content delivery system. Only human visitors will see this page. Automated bots and crawlers will be served other, unique content.

This application is designed to identify different types of visitors, such as search engine crawlers and AI bots, and provide them with very specific segments of information. By doing so, we can further optimize our SEO efforts and ensure that our content is being crawled and scraped in an ethical way and by the right audience.

Check out our other site here!

How This Site Works:

  • Identifies visitors based on their User-Agent by extracting just the bot name.
  • Serves a unique, dynamically generated page to that specific bot from a given set of templates.
  • If a human user finds this site, we provide this standard, friendly page to them.
